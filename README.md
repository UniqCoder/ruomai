# 🚀 Ruom - AI Content Repurposing for Global Creators

**Ruom** is an AI-powered content repurposing web application built specifically for Indian creators, coaches, and consultants. It takes a single piece of long-form content—such as a blog post, YouTube transcript, podcast notes, or even a raw idea—and transforms it into platform-ready outputs in under 10 seconds.

## ✨ The Vision
Instead of spending hours rewriting the same content for Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram Reels, WhatsApp, and email newsletters, simply paste your content once, select your desired output formats, choose your tone, and click **Ruom It**. We handle the rest.

The name **Ruom** is derived from its founder, Om Bhirud ("Ru" from Bhirud, "Om" from Om) and carries the phonetics of reach and spread in Sanskrit and Marathi. The goal is simple: make "Ruom it" a verb for distributing content everywhere effortlessly.

## 🎯 The Problem
Indian creators—coaches, educators, fitness influencers, and consultants—face a daily distribution bottleneck:
- **Time Constraints:** Repurposing a 20-minute video into multiple formats takes 2-4 hours of manual work.
- **Fragmented Reach:** Most creators stick to one or two platforms, leaving audience engagement and revenue on the table.
- **Prompt Fatigue:** Generic AI tools require complex prompt engineering, intimidating non-technical users.
- **Western Bias:** Existing tools (like Buffer or Taplio) are built for Western markets, charge in USD, and fail to understand Hinglish communication or Indian creator culture.

## 💡 The Solution
Ruom solves the repurposing problem with radical simplicity. 

**What Ruom generates from one input:**
- 🧵 **Tweet Thread:** 5 hook-driven tweets, formatted for high engagement.
- 💼 **LinkedIn Post:** Professional yet personal posts tailored for the Indian B2B audience.
- 🎬 **Reel Script:** A 30-second script complete with a hook, body, and CTA.
- 📧 **Newsletter Intro:** Opening hooks designed to grab subscriber attention instantly.
- 💬 **WhatsApp Broadcast:** Short, conversational messages (under 200 words) for community groups.

**Key Differentiators:**
- 🇮🇳 **Hindi & Hinglish Toggle:** The only tool that speaks the true language of Indian creators.
- 🎭 **Tone Selector:** Choose between Professional, Casual, Witty, or Inspirational.
- 💸 **INR & UPI Pricing:** Frictionless payments via Razorpay.
- 📱 **Mobile-First:** Designed for creators who run their business from their phones.
- 🧠 **Zero Prompting:** No blank pages, no overcomplicated dashboards.

## 🏰 Our Moat
Ruom's competitive advantage isn't just AI—it's specificity and deep localization.
1. **Language Moat:** Unmatched Hindi and Hinglish output that generic Western tools ignore.
2. **Niche Positioning:** Tailored for Indian platform priorities (e.g., WhatsApp communities over Slack).
3. **Tone Memory:** The more you use Ruom, the more it learns your exact writing style.
4. **Brand Moat:** Building a category-defining tool where creators say, *"Just Ruom your video."*
